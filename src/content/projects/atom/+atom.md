A two-panel terminal file explorer built with **C++** and **ncurses**. Left panel lists directories, right panel lists files — navigate entirely with keyboard shortcuts, auto-launch files in the right app (`Zed`, `VSCode`, `Chrome`, or `xdg-open`), and have your shell `cd` into wherever you stopped browsing when you quit.

No mouse. No config files. No background services. Just direct interaction with the filesystem from inside your terminal.

Read the [blog post](/blog/atom) for the full write-up on how it was built.
